ENGINE TUNNING OPTIONS

Our engine tuning is performed by APP Racing Engines. All engines, as other features of the project, can be fully cusomized by order. Theese are the main options.

Upgrade 3.4

Classic 911 3.2 modified with cylinder set and forged piston set. Offers 260hp and 320Nm.

964/993 3.6

Equiped with piston rings, rod bearingm, rod bolts and nuts and intake and exhaust valves, each with valve springs. Offers 285hp and 350Nm.

964/993 3.9 RS

Rennsport engine. High performance features such as a cylinder set custom 3.9L 104mm, titanium retainers and springseats, double valve springs set, and a 996 GT3 oilpump. Offers 320hp adn 415Nm.

964/993 4.0 RSII

Second generation RS engine with the camshaft upgrade L+R custom grind added. Offers 340hp and 440Nm.

930T 3.3

Equiped with a K27 Turbocharger, an intake valve 51mm, and a camshaft upgrade. Offers 380hp and 500Nm.

964T 3.6

Forged pistons, cylinder and rings. K27 Turbocharger, turbo flywheel kit, intercooler, and boost gauge. Offers 400hp and 500Nm.
